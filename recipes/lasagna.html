Lasagna

Description

This delicious and cheesy lasagna will be on your table in forty minutes or less!

Ingredients

  • 16 oz lasagna noodles
  • 26 oz spaghetti sauce
  • 16 oz cottage cheese
  • 8 oz shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 8 oz shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F
  2. Bring a large pot of water to a boil and cook the lasagna noodles
  3. Layer noodles, sauce, and cheese
  4. Bake in preheated oven 30 to 45 minutes
