Cake Mix Cookies

Description

Easy and delicious three-ingredient cookies!

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 package white cake mix
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Mix together cake mix, eggs, and oil in a large bowl
  2. Make little balls with the dough and set on ungreased baking sheets
  3. Bake at 350 degrees for 10 minutes
