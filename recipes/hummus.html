Black Bean Hummus

Description

A healthy and delicious snack to help you keep your New Year's Resolutions!

Ingredients

  • 1 clove garlic
  • 15 oz black beans
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1.5 tablespoons tahini
  • .75 teaspoon cumin
  • .5 teaspoon salt
  • .25 teaspoon paprika
  • 10 Greek olives

Steps

  1. Add all ingredients to the bowl of a food processor and process until smooth
